# DD. Unspecified Behavior

According to the C Standard, Annex J, J.1 \[ [ISO/IEC 9899:2011](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/back-matter/aa-bibliography#AA.Bibliography-ISO/IEC9899-2011) \], the behavior of a program is [unspecified](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/back-matter/bb-definitions#BB.Definitions-unspecifiedbehavior) in the circumstances outlined the following table. The descriptions of unspecified behaviors in the "Description" column are direct quotes from the standard. The parenthesized numbers refer to the subclause of the C Standard (C11) that identifies the unspecified behavior. The "Guideline" column in the table identifies the coding practices that address the specific case of unspecified behavior (USB).
