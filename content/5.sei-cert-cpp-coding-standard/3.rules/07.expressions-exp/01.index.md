The following rules from the [SEI CERT C Coding Standard](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/) also apply in C++:

-   [EXP34-C. Do not dereference null pointers](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/expressions-exp/exp34-c)
-   [EXP35-C. Do not modify objects with temporary lifetime](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/expressions-exp/exp35-c)
-   [EXP36-C. Do not cast pointers into more strictly aligned pointer types](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/expressions-exp/exp36-c)

-   [EXP39-C. Do not access a variable through a pointer of an incompatible type](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/expressions-exp/exp39-c)
-   [EXP42-C. Do not compare padding data](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/expressions-exp/exp42-c)
-   [EXP45-C. Do not perform assignments in selection statements](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/expressions-exp/exp45-c)
-   [EXP46-C. Do not use a bitwise operator with a Boolean-like operand](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/expressions-exp/exp46-c)
-   [EXP47-C. Do not call va_arg with an argument of the incorrect type](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/expressions-exp/exp47-c)
