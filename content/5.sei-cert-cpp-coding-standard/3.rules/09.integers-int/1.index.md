-   [INT50-CPP. Do not cast to an out-of-range enumeration value](/sei-cert-cpp-coding-standard/rules/integers-int/int50-cpp)

  

The following rules from the [SEI CERT C Coding Standard](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/) also apply in C++:

-   [INT30-C. Ensure that unsigned integer operations do not wrap](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/integers-int/int30-c)
-   [INT31-C. Ensure that integer conversions do not result in lost or misinterpreted data](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/integers-int/int31-c)
-   [INT32-C. Ensure that operations on signed integers do not result in overflow](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/integers-int/int32-c)
-   [INT33-C. Ensure that division and remainder operations do not result in divide-by-zero errors](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/integers-int/int33-c)
-   [INT34-C. Do not shift an expression by a negative number of bits or by greater than or equal to the number of bits that exist in the operand](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/integers-int/int34-c)
-   [INT35-C. Use correct integer precisions](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/integers-int/int35-c)
-   [INT36-C. Converting a pointer to integer or integer to pointer](/sei-cert-c-coding-standard/rules/integers-int/int36-c)
